What We're Looking For
  • Suppressed or retracted peer-reviewed studies
  • Negative trial data buried by pharma
  • NIH / FDA grant denials citing political reasons
  • Researchers defunded for inconvenient findings
  • FOIA documents revealing institutional suppression
  • Whistleblower accounts with documentation
  • Promising research ignored because no patent exists
  • Wellness claims with strong evidence being dismissed
What Makes a Strong Tip
  • Named individuals and institutions
  • Primary source documentation
  • Clear description of what was suppressed and why
  • Documented patient or public health impact
  • Timeline of events
